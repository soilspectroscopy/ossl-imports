Part of: https://github.com/soilspectroscopy
Project: Soil Spectroscopy for Global Good
Last update: 2021-12-04
Dataset: ICRAF.ISRIC

The ICRAF-ISRIC Soil and Spectral Library (Garrity & Bindraban, 2004) has samples from 58 countries worldwide including 5 continents (Africa, Europe, South America, North America, and Asia). The soil samples were retrieved from the Soil Information System (ISIS), which already had the analytical data, and the spectra were analysed in the World Agroforestry Centres (ICRAF). Dataset properties and licences are explained in detail in ISRIC data portal.

Input datasets:

  • ICRAF_ISRIC_MIR_spectra.csv: MIR soil spectral reflectances (>3578 channels);
  • ICRAF_ISRIC_VNIR_spectra.csv: VNIR soil spectral reflectances (>216 channels);
  • ICRAF_ISRIC_reference_data.csv: Database with site and soil analytes;

For the ICRAF-ISRIC dataset properties refer also to Aitkenhead & Black (2018).
