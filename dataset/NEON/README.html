Mid-Infrared Spectra (MIRS) from The National Ecological Observatory Network (NEON) Soil Spectral Library explained in detail in Dangal & Sanderman (2020). NEON data standards are explained in detail in Ayres (2019).

Soil site and laboratory data import:

Get coordinates (download from https://data.neonscience.org/data-products/explore). Metadata is available in tabular format.
