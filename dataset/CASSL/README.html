Part of: https://github.com/soilspectroscopy
Project: Soil Spectroscopy for Global Good
Last update: 2022-05-08
Dataset: CAF.SSL

Mid-Infrared Spectra (MIRS) from The central African soil spectral library explained in detail in Summerauer et al. (2021). A copy of the dataset can be obtain from https://github.com/laura-summerauer/ssl-central-africa.
